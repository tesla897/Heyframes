## Style Prompt

Bold editorial explainer cards with a warm paper canvas, heavy black ink borders, oversized uppercase headlines, simple geometric marks, and restrained accent color. The piece should feel like a clean YouTube explainer frame system: readable, punchy, graphic, and sturdy.

## Colors

- Ink: #111111
- Paper: #f8f8f3
- Warm background: #f3f0e8
- Neutral background: #f5f5f5
- Gold accent: #f4c542
- Red accent: #e05454
- Violet accent: #7f65d6

## Typography

- Trebuchet MS for the comic explainer voice
- Segoe UI as the system fallback

## What NOT to Do

- Do not use gradients, glossy effects, or cinematic realism.
- Do not use thin borders or delicate type.
- Do not let decorative shapes compete with the headline.
- Do not add extra explanatory UI text outside the composition itself.
